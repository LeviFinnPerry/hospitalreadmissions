# hospitalreadmissions
Investigating hospital readmissions in diabetic patients

Using the dataset https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/brandao/diabetes

Hospitals aim to efficently treat patients during times of acute injury or illness.
The best way of seeing if a hospital is effectively treating patients is to examine
the readmission rate. If a patient is returning soon after treatment, it is likely that
the treatment was ineffective or a condition is left diagnosed.

Diabetic patients are a significant portion of hospital admissions, from amputations
to prescribing medications to manage the condition.
